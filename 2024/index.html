Rijnlands Lyceum Oegstgeest
Model United Nations 2024

11th to 13th October 2024

Dear MUN directors, advisors, delegates, student officers, admins, and guests,

On behalf of the RijnMUN 2024 Board of Directors, welcome to the archive of RijnMUN 2024.
The inaugural RijnMUN 2024 conference was a massive success, with 170 delegates and over 60 volunteers supporting the conference. We thank everyone who was present and made it possible.
Below, you will find a record of the first ever RijnMUN.
